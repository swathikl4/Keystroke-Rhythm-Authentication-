Common questions

Here are some of the most common questions that we get.
What is keystroke dynamics authentication? Keystroke dynamics authentication is a biometric authentication method that analyzes the unique typing patterns of individuals to verify their identity.
How secure is keystroke dynamics authentication? Keystroke dynamics authentication is considered a highly secure method as it relies on individual typing patterns which are difficult to replicate or fake.
Can keystroke dynamics authentication be used for all users? Keystroke dynamics authentication may not be suitable for users with certain disabilities or those who have inconsistent typing patterns.
Is keystroke dynamics authentication user-friendly? Keystroke dynamics authentication can be user-friendly once users get accustomed to the process of typing for authentication.
How accurate is keystroke dynamics authentication? Keystroke dynamics authentication can be highly accurate in verifying user identities based on their unique typing patterns.
